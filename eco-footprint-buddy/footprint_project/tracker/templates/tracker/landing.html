About Eco Footprint Buddy

Eco Footprint Buddy helps you track your daily carbon emissions and gives you tips to reduce your footprint. You answer simple questions, and it calculates your carbon score.
This is a full fledged Python Full Stack Project created by Arijit Patra, Dibyaranjan Prusty, Daikho Athishu and T.Lalremsangi.
And Special thanks to LPU for their support and special thanks to Sir, under his teaching and guidance we were able to create this project.
